Dolls have been popular toys for children for centuries. They come in different shapes, sizes, and materials. Some dolls are made of plastic, while others are made of cloth, wood, or porcelain. Dolls can be dressed up in various outfits and accessories, and they can even have their hair styled. Playing with dolls can be beneficial for children's development. It helps in developing their imagination, creativity, and social skills. Children can use dolls to act out different scenarios, which can help them in problem-solving and critical thinking. Playing with dolls can also help children in understanding emotions and developing empathy. There are different types of dolls available in the market, from baby dolls to fashion dolls. Baby dolls can be used to teach children about nurturing and caring for others. Fashion dolls can help children develop an interest in fashion and design. Dolls can also be used as collectibles for adults. Some people believe that dolls can be haunted or possess negative energy.
