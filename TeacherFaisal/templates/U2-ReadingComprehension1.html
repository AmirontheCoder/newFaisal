3) Read the following passage then answer the questions that follow. (External)

Mark Twain once said: “Eat alive frog in the morning and nothing worse will happen to you the rest of the day” . For self-help development, this quote serves as a metaphor for effective time management. It means simply you should tackle the hardest and most important task on your to-do list every morning. , so you’re likely to take on a positive approach about the rest of your day.

There are lots of tasks that are most likely to be classified as “frogs” . Some tasks are classified as difficult tasks, others as important tasks , and some others are both difficult and important. A difficult task is that which you keep putting off because it would take too much of your time, or because it is simply boring. For example, this may be an extensive research for a project. An important task is that which needs you to fully focus on it for a longer period of time like cost and time estimates you have to set for an elusive project. And if you’re really out of luck, your task can be both important and difficult ,or in other words, boring and crucial for your work like doing 200-word abstract for a 50-page project research – with the deadline being mere hours away.

Correct Answer : What tasks can be frogs

If you have to eat two frogs, begin with the ugly frog . This is another way of saying that if you have two important tasks before you, start with the biggest, hardest, and most important task first. The gist to the “eat that frog” time management technique is simple. First, you’ll need to create a list of tasks you have to do .Then label your tasks according to their level of importance and urgency to identify your “frog” . Work on your “frog” first thing in the morning and remember to persist until the task is complete. Once done, shift your focus to less crucial tasks in your schedule.

Correct Answer : How to eat the frog

Everyone has a certain type of tasks they don’t enjoy. Doing the most difficult or the most important task first thing in the morning will have a great positive impact on the rest of your day. This little achievement will help energize and motivate you, and you will glide through the rest of the day because you’ll know that all the other tasks that wait you are easier. It makes prioritizing easier as you’ll be more likely to recognize what tasks aren’t important, what tasks you can discard entirely and what tasks you should really focus on. It leaves you with more time for enjoyable tasks.

Correct Answer : Advantages of eating the frog
