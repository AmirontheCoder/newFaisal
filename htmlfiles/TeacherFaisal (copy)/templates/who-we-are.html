About Us

Welcome to the “Teacher Faisal” online platform.

Welcome to the “Teacher Faisal” online platform. If you are a twelfth grader in Palestine ready to embark on the Tawjihi journey, this site is where you should be.

Over the past few months, we have embarked on a transformative journey through the new AI revolution, coinciding with the widespread transition of schools to e-learning systems and resources. Inspired by these advancements, we have innovatively combined these ideas to create a platform that addresses a major challenge in Palestine – excelling in the Tawjihi English exam.

Our unique features A comprehensive range of resources to help students enhance their reading comprehension, vocabulary, grammar, and writing abilities, all powered by the AI model. The opportunity for students to engage in interactive discussions with “Teacher Faisal”, who is ready to provide clear explanations or create discussions as needed by the student. A curated list of external resources, including an online library of documents, quizzes, and previous English Tawjihi exams to further enrich students' learning experience. A special dictionary feature allows students to search for the meaning of any unfamiliar word, providing both Arabic and English definitions. .

Best of luck and we hope that this platform can help you in the Best Possible way!

Note: Our platform Teacher Faisal is named after Faisal Abdel Al Qader Al Hussein (1940-2001), Palestinian politician, human rights defender, an intellectual and a co-founder of the Arab Studies Society. This platform is in his memory, for his love for Jerusalem and comprehensive vision for educated Palestinian generations to come.
